Our Story

Why WebVibes Technology Started?

Born from a simple observation — freshers with degrees but no job-ready skills

2000+ Students Placed

Every year, thousands of engineering graduates step out of college with degrees in hand but struggle to land their first job. Not because they aren't smart — but because the gap between academic learning and industry expectations is massive.

Our founder witnessed this firsthand. Brilliant students were being rejected not for lack of intelligence, but for lack of practical, hands-on skills that companies actually need on day one.

WebVibes Technology was built to bridge that gap — to turn freshers into job-ready professionals through real project experience, mentorship, and industry-aligned curriculum.
